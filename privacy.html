Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our application ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser-based services and any associated Google Drive synchronizations.

1. Data Storage & Ownership

Our application is fundamentally built as a client-side first application. This means:

  • We do not operate proprietary, centralized database servers to host your content.
  • Your project files, media, and animation states are stored locally on your device (within your browser's local storage) by default.
  • Because data is processed locally, we do not monitor, read, or evaluate the contents of your creative projects.

2. Google Drive Integration

If you choose to enable cloud synchronization for cross-device access and backup, you must authenticate via Google OAuth. Here is how we handle your Google Data:

  • Restricted Scope: We strictly request the drive.file OAuth scope. This is a restricted, highly specific permission context.
  • Limited Access: The drive.file scope means our application can only access, view, and modify files that it has created itself. It is mathematically impossible for us to access, read, or modify your general Google Drive contents (e.g., your personal documents, photos, or spreadsheets).
  • Sandboxed Directory: All your synced data is safely sandboxed within a dedicated application folder automatically created in your Google Drive.
  • Your Control: You maintain full ownership and control of your export data and can revoke this app's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account Security settings.

3. Third-party Services & Data Sharing

We believe in strict privacy:

  • We do not sell, rent, or distribute any user metrics, files, or personal information to third parties or marketing agencies.
  • No third-party tracking pixels or pervasive advertising SDKs are embedded in the application.

4. User Consent

By using our service and explicitly clicking "Allow" on the Google OAuth consent screen, you are opting into Google Drive synchronization operations necessary to store files on your behalf. You may disconnect or revoke this access at any time.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will notify you of any material changes via a notice in the application.
