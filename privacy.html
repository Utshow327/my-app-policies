Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

DrawAnimator ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information as follows:

1. Data Storage: Your files are stored locally on your device or in your personal Google Drive. We do not operate our own database servers to store your work.

2. Google Drive: We use the restricted 'drive.file' scope to save your animations into a specific DrawAnimator folder on your Google Drive.

3. Third Parties: We do not sell or share your data.
