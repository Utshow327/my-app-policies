Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our video editing and captioning application. We prioritize your privacy and minimize data collection.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Our app operates primarily on-device to ensure your data stays private:

  • Local Processing: Videos, audio, and projects are processed and stored locally on your device's storage. We do not upload your video library to our servers.
  • AI Services: To generate captions, audio data may be routed securely through third-party AI transcription services. This data is transmitted instantly for transcription only and is not retained or used for training models.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous crash reports and minimal app usage statistics to fix bugs and improve performance. No personally identifiable information is attached to this data.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your generated content and projects are saved locally on your device, you possess total control. You can permanently delete all associated data by clearing the app data in your device settings or uninstalling the application.

4. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party APIs solely to facilitate operations like AI transcription. These providers receive only the minimum necessary data to perform their function and are bound by strict data security standards.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time to remain compliant with changing legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us via the support email listed on our Google Play Store page.
