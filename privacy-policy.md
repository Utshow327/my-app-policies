This Privacy Policy explains how DrawAnimator ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Data Storage & Ownership: DrawAnimator is a client-side application. We do not operate proprietary database servers. Your project files, media, and animation states are stored locally on your device or synchronized securely to your personal Google Drive account.

2. Google Drive Integration: If you choose to enable cloud synchronization, you must authenticate via Google OAuth. We request restricted access (drive.file scope) limited strictly to the "DrawAnimator" application folder. We cannot access your general Google Drive contents. You maintain full ownership and control of your export data.

3. Third-party Services: We do not sell, rent, or distribute any user metrics, files, or personal information to third parties.

4. User Consent: By using DrawAnimator and opting into Google Drive synchronization, you consent to the operations necessary to store files on your behalf. You may revoke this access at any time from your Google Account settings.
